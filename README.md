# TinyCom

A very small and low cost computer, made in KiCAD.
(work in progress...)

## Specifications

- Dimensions: 4x4 cm
- CPU: Allwinner A13
- Memory: 512MB DDR3 SDRAM
- PCB: 4 layers, 6 mil traces, 12 mil vias
- Ports: SD-card, serial, USB, camera
